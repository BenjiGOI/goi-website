Our Story

The Global Ophthalmic Institute was born out of the challenges observed during the COVID-19 pandemic. It became clear that there was a critical lack of high-quality, comprehensive educational resources accessible to eye care professionals globally. Understanding that in-person support isn't always possible, we are leveraging technology and online resources to bridge this gap and support our colleagues around the world.

Our Mission

To facilitate access for the global eye health workforce to quality eye health education resources, thereby strengthening professional training and improving the provision and standards of patient care.
