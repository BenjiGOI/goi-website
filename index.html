About Our Mission

The Global Ophthalmic Institute is dedicated to eliminating preventable blindness and providing world-class eye care to communities that need it most.

Global Reach

Operating in over 30 countries, bringing eye care to remote and underserved areas.

Expert Care

World-renowned ophthalmologists and medical professionals dedicated to excellence.

Compassionate Mission

Driven by compassion and the belief that sight is a fundamental human right.

Our Services

Comprehensive eye care services designed to prevent, treat, and cure vision problems.

Eye Examinations

Comprehensive eye health assessments and vision screenings.

Cataract Surgery

Advanced surgical procedures to restore clear vision.

Vision Correction

Prescription glasses and contact lens fittings.

Training Programs

Education and training for local healthcare providers.
